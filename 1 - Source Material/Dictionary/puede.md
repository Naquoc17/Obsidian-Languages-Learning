- puede	[ˈpwe.ðe]	Verb (third person singular, present)
- Meaning (explicación en español): Verbo “poder” en tercera persona
- Meaning (Vietnamese): (cô ấy/anh ấy) có thể
- Properly translated word: can (he/she can)
